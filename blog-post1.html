Workout Routines

Explore our expertly crafted workout routines tailored to help you meet your fitness goals!

Cardio Routine

Frequency: 3 times a week

Duration: 30-45 minutes

Exercises:

  • Warm-up: 5-10 minutes brisk walking or jogging
  • High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT):
    • 30 seconds sprinting
    • 1-minute walking (repeat for 15-20 minutes)
  • Cool-down: 5-10 minutes walking and stretching

Strength Training Routine

Frequency: 2-3 times a week (non-consecutive days)

Duration: 45-60 minutes

Exercises:

  • Warm-up: 5-10 minutes dynamic stretching
  • Circuit (3 sets of 10-15 reps each):
    • Squats
    • Push-ups
    • Bent-over rows
    • Lunges
    • Plank (hold for 30 seconds)
  • Cool-down: 5-10 minutes stretching

Yoga Routine

Frequency: 1-2 times a week

Duration: 30-60 minutes

Exercises:

  • Sun Salutations (5 rounds)
  • Warrior I and II (hold each for 30 seconds)
  • Downward Dog (hold for 30 seconds)
  • Tree Pose (hold for 30 seconds each side)
  • Seated Forward Bend (hold for 1-2 minutes)
  • Relaxation: Savasana (5-10 minutes)

Pilates Routine

Frequency: 1-2 times a week

Duration: 30-45 minutes

Exercises:

  • Warm-up: 5 minutes of breathing exercises
  • Circuit (2-3 sets of 10-15 reps each):
    • The Hundred
    • Roll-Up
    • Leg Circles
    • Single Leg Stretch
    • Plank (hold for 30 seconds)
  • Cool-down: 5-10 minutes stretching

Zumba Routine

Frequency: 2 times a week

Duration: 30-45 minutes

Exercises:

  • Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light dancing
  • Dance routine: 20-30 minutes of high-energy dance (e.g., salsa, merengue)
  • Cool-down: 5-10 minutes of gentle dancing and stretching

HIIT Routine

Frequency: 2 times a week

Duration: 30-45 minutes

Exercises:

  • Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio
  • Intervals:
    • 1 minute of high-intensity exercise (e.g., burpees, jump squats)
    • 30 seconds of rest
    • Repeat for 20-30 minutes
  • Cool-down: 5-10 minutes of stretching
