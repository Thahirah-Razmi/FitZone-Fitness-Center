Healthy Meal Plans

Choose a meal plan that suits your dietary needs and fitness goals!

Plan A: Balanced Diet

This plan focuses on a balanced intake of carbs, proteins, and fats.

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with fruits
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad
  • Dinner: Salmon with quinoa and vegetables
  • Snacks: Almonds and Greek yogurt

Plan B: High Protein

This plan is designed for those looking to increase muscle mass.

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach
  • Lunch: Turkey wrap with veggies
  • Dinner: Grilled steak with sweet potatoes
  • Snacks: Cottage cheese and mixed nuts

Plan C: Vegetarian

A delicious plan for those who prefer a meat-free diet.

  • Breakfast: Smoothie bowl with berries
  • Lunch: Chickpea salad with feta
  • Dinner: Vegetable stir-fry with tofu
  • Snacks: Hummus with carrot sticks
